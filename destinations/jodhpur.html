Welcome to the Blue City

Discover the vibrant culture and iconic landmarks of Jodhpur.

Mehrangarh Fort

Standing tall atop a hill, Mehrangarh Fort is a majestic structure that offers stunning views of Jodhpur. Explore its museum, intricate carvings, and the history that defines this fort.

Jaswant Thada

A white marble cenotaph built in memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II, Jaswant Thada is often called the "Taj Mahal of Marwar" and is surrounded by tranquil gardens.

Umaid Bhawan Palace

One of the world’s largest private residences, the Umaid Bhawan Palace is a magnificent example of Indo-European architecture. Part of it is now a luxury hotel.

Mandore Gardens

These historic gardens feature cenotaphs of Marwar rulers, ancient temples, and lush green landscapes, making it a perfect spot for history buffs and nature lovers.
