Additional Items: Travel power strip, E-reader or books, Portable Wi-Fi device or SIM card

3. Clothing

- Weather-appropriate clothing (based on destination climate)
- Comfortable walking shoes (essential for sightseeing)
- Casual outfits for daytime activities
- Swimwear (for beach vacations or hotel pools)
- Sleepwear, Undergarments, and socks

Additional Items: Flip-flops, Hat, sunglasses, Laundry bag
