Flight Booking Tips

1. Book Early for Cheaper Fares

For international flights, try to book 2-3 months in advance. For domestic flights, 1-2 months is often the sweet spot for finding cheaper fares.

- Last-minute deals:

If you're flexible, some airlines offer last-minute deals, though this is less common for long-haul flights.

Tip: Set price alerts to get notified when fares drop!

2. Be Flexible with Dates

Tuesdays and Wednesdays tend to have the lowest fares, while weekends (Friday-Sunday) are typically more expensive.

- Avoid peak seasons:

If possible, avoid flying during holidays, school vacations, or popular travel seasons. Off-peak travel can offer significant savings.

Tip: Booking flights for Tuesday or Wednesday mornings can often be cheaper than flying on weekends!
